About Paige

A little bit about me

Hi, I'm Paige!

I'm an Ottawa-based wedding and portrait photographer with a passion for capturing real, unscripted moments. My ultimate goal is to make you feel completely comfortable — no stiff poses, no awkward directions. Just you, your people, and the beautiful emotions of your day.

Whether it's a grand wedding celebration or an intimate couple session, I'm here to document it honestly, warmly, and with so much heart.
